DropMe – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

DropMe is a peer-to-peer file transfer application that lets you securely share files between devices using a shared encryption key. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information DropMe handles, how it is protected, and your rights as a user.

Information We Do Not Collect

DropMe is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • The files you transfer
  • Your encryption keys
  • Your personal identity, name, or email address
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Any metadata about transferred files (names, sizes, or types)

How File Transfers Work

All file transfers take place directly between the devices involved in the session. Files are encrypted on your device before transmission using the shared encryption key you provide. No file content or encryption key is ever sent to or stored on our servers.

Once a transfer session ends, no data is retained by the application.

Encryption & Security

DropMe uses strong symmetric encryption to protect your files in transit. The shared encryption key is known only to the participants in the transfer session and is never transmitted to or stored by DropMe. You are responsible for sharing the encryption key securely with the intended recipient.

Third-Party Services

DropMe does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If future versions of the app include optional third-party integrations (for example, for signalling or relay services), this policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be informed before any such service is used.

Children's Privacy

DropMe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used DropMe in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how DropMe handles data, please contact us at: privacy@dropme.app
